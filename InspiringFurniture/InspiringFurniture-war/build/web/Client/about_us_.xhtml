About Us

Inspiring Furniture company was established in 2021, in Can Tho, Vietnam, by four team members. We are a fresh start-up company and we aim to be among the country’s largest furniture companies in the future.


Life experience and house decoration gradually become a part of people’s daily lives, nowadays. Besides living in decent residences, making them personalized and self-aesthetic are what people going after. Therefore, Inspiring Furniture company was founded to accommodate those needs.


The company has several storages across the country which assure to have stable furniture supply for local customers. Furniture from the company is not only affordable, environment-friendly but also has various models and materials to be chosen from. Mainly, we are invested in furniture for the living room and wall decoration, in particular.


Our furniture provides plenty of choices in materials for a wide selection of products. Materials are carefully and considerably selected to guarantee that we bring top-notch products to our customers. We have products that are made from wood, glass, iron, bronze, etc… Last but not least, products are fairly affordable, just a tidy sum, and customers would get what they deserve best.
